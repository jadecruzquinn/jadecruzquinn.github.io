# TurboTax voice & tone

**Source:** https://contentdesign.intuit.com/voice-tone/turbotax/

---

## TurboTax brand character: your neighborhood tax visionary

The neighborhood tax visionary is relentlessly in the customer's corner, reimagining what taxes look like with an optimistic and forward-thinking approach.

To do that, our tone is **down to earth**, **honestly insightful**, and **contagiously confident**.

---

## TurboTax voice principles

### Down to earth

Taxes are intimidating, but we aren't. We put our customers at ease by rooting ourselves in service, letting them know we're here for them no matter their comfort level. But don't try hard with slang.

| In spontaneous contexts | In structured contexts |
|-------------------------|------------------------|
| Ground in culture | Call it what it is |
| Be current | Simplify |
| Add humor | Be friendly |

### Honestly insightful

Our tone is radically honest, looking out for our customers' best interests. Let's enable the wins with actionable insights and empower despite challenging realities. But don't be blunt or navel gaze.

| In spontaneous contexts | In structured contexts |
|-------------------------|------------------------|
| Say it straight | Offer proactive solutions |
| Have their back | Quantify the benefit |
| Lead with empathy | Translate jargon |

### Contagiously confident

We're the experts assuring customers that they're in good hands. We fuel financial progress with purposeful innovations, giving them a boost to make bold choices. But don't be condescending or go overboard with exclamation marks.

| In spontaneous contexts | In structured contexts |
|-------------------------|------------------------|
| Tell audacious stories | Give customers control |
| Be at ease | Back up claims |
| Have swagger | Speak with authority |
